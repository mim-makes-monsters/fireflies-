Why "Fireflies"?

I used to catch fireflies as a kid, cup them in my hands, watch them blink for a few seconds, then let them go. They never stayed long. That's the whole reason for the name — these are kids who were here for such a short time, and then weren't.

I started this because I kept reading about cases like Summer Wells or Dulce Alavez somewhere online, getting that gut-punch feeling, and then a week later forgetting their names. That bothered me. Not because I think I owe anyone constant grief, but because forgetting feels like the last thing that should happen to them.

This isn't a tip line and it's not trying to solve anything — NCMEC and local police do that work, and they're better equipped for it than I am. I just wanted one page where the names don't disappear under the next news cycle. Some of these kids came home. A lot didn't. A few, honestly, nobody really knows what happened, and I think that not-knowing deserves space too.

If something here is wrong, tell me. I'd rather fix it than leave it.
